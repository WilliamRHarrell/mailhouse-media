## Account Structure: Single Account (Recommended)

**Use one GHL account for all Mailhouse Media operations across all cities.**

### Why Single Account

✅ **Unified view** — See all leads across all cities in one dashboard  
✅ **Simpler management** — No switching between accounts  
✅ **Better reporting** — Aggregate analytics across all markets  
✅ **Cross-city insights** — Spot trends, compare performance  
✅ **Easier team management** — One login for your team  
✅ **Clean separation** — Mailhouse Media is its own account (not mixed with ATS sub-accounts)  

### Organization Strategy

**Tags** (primary organization):
- City tags: `city-fayetteville`, `city-jacksonville`, `city-raleigh`
- Route tags: `route-1`, `route-2`, `route-3`
- Status tags: `status-new`, `status-contacted`, `status-paid`
- Source tags: `source-google-maps`, `source-website`

**Custom Fields** (data storage):
- `city` — Fayetteville, Jacksonville, etc.
- `routeNumber` — 1, 2, 3, etc.
- `zipCode` — 28301, 28303, etc.
- `adSpaceStatus` — available, reserved, paid, mailed

**Smart Lists** (filtered views):
- "Fayetteville - All Leads" → filter by tag: city-fayetteville
- "Route 1 - Fayetteville" → filter by tag: city-fayetteville + route-1
- "Paid - All Cities" → filter by tag: status-paid
- "Needs Follow-Up" → filter by tag: status-contacted + no reply

**Pipelines** (sales tracking):
- Single pipeline: "Ad Space Sales"
- Stages: New → Contacted → Interested → Reserved → Paid → Ad Received → Mailed
- Filter pipeline view by city tag

### Reporting by City

Use GHL's built-in reporting with tag filters:

**Contacts Report:**
- Filter by tag: `city-fayetteville`
- See: total contacts, conversion rate, revenue

**Pipeline Report:**
- Filter by tag: `city-raleigh`
- See: deals in each stage, win rate, avg deal size

**Revenue Report:**
- Group by custom field: `city`
- See: revenue per city, per month

### Example Workflow

**Viewing Fayetteville Performance:**
1. Go to Contacts
2. Click Smart List: "Fayetteville - All Leads"
3. See all Fayetteville contacts
4. Filter by pipeline stage
5. View conversion rates, revenue

**Viewing All Paid Customers:**
1. Go to Contacts
2. Click Smart List: "Paid - All Cities"
3. See all paid customers across all cities
4. Export list for ad creative collection

**Comparing City Performance:**
1. Go to Reports
2. Filter by tag: `city-fayetteville` → save report
3. Filter by tag: `city-jacksonville` → save report
4. Compare side-by-side

## Two Big Opportunities

### 1. SaaS Mode (Passive Income)

**What it is:** White-label GHL and resell it to other operators as a subscription service.

**How it works:**
1. You build the Mailhouse Media system (workflows, templates, processes)
2. You license it to operators in other cities
3. They pay you $497/month for access
4. They run the business in their city
5. You collect recurring revenue without doing the work

**Revenue model:**
```
You provide:
- GHL access (pre-configured workflows/templates)
- n8n scraping workflows
- Email/SMS templates
- Landing page template
- Training/support

Operator provides:
- Local market knowledge
- Sales effort
- Ad creative collection
- Printer relationship

You charge: $497/month per city
They keep: 100% of ad sales
```

**Scaling math:**
- 10 cities @ $497/mo = $4,970/month ($59,640/year)
- 20 cities @ $497/mo = $9,940/month ($119,280/year)
- 50 cities @ $497/mo = $24,850/month ($298,200/year)
- 100 cities @ $497/mo = $49,700/month ($596,400/year)

**This is how you build a $1M+ business without doing fulfillment.**

**When to use:**
- After you've proven the model in 3-5 cities
- When you have documented processes
- When you're ready to scale without operational overhead

**Setup time:** 2-3 hours to configure SaaS mode

### 2. Unlimited Team Members (Operational Scale)

**What it is:** Add unlimited team members to your GHL account.

**How it works:**
- Hire sales reps, city managers, designers, etc.
- Give them access to the single Mailhouse Media account
- Set permissions (admin, user, readonly)
- Track their performance

**Team structure example:**
```
Ryan (Owner/Admin)
├── Sales Rep 1 (handles Fayetteville + Jacksonville)
├── Sales Rep 2 (handles Raleigh + Charlotte)
├── City Manager (oversees all NC cities)
├── Designer (ad creative for all cities)
└── VA (customer support, ad collection)
```

**Benefits:**
- Scale without bottlenecks
- Delegate operations
- Track team performance
- Hire specialists

**When to use:**
- When you're doing 5+ routes/month
- When you're spending too much time on operations
- When you want to focus on strategy vs execution
